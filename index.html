Longxi Li

Professor and Doctoral Supervisor

个人简介

Li Longxi, Professor and Doctoral Supervisor. He graduated from Dalian University of Technology in 2017 with a Ph.D. degree. Dr. Li is currently the Deputy Director of the Energy and Environmental Management and Decision-Making Research Center and the Deputy Director of the Department of Management Science and Engineering at China University of Geosciences (Wuhan). He is recognized as an Outstanding Young Talent and a member of the Young Top-notch Talent Program at China University of Geosciences (Wuhan).

学术职位

  • Executive Committee Member of the Energy Resource System Engineering Special Committee, Chinese Systems Engineering Society
  • Council Member of the Energy Economics and Management Branch, Chinese Society for Optimization, Overall Planning and Economic Mathematics
  • Committee Member of the Data Science and Knowledge System Engineering Special Committee, Chinese Systems Engineering Society

研究领域

His primary research focuses on integrated energy services, smart energy management, data-driven energy system optimization and decision-making, and coordinated operation of energy and transportation systems.

研究项目

  • National Natural Science Foundation of China General Project
  • Sub-project of the National Social Science Foundation of China Major Project
  • National Natural Science Foundation of China Youth Project
  • Hubei Provincial Natural Science Foundation Project

He has also participated in major projects such as the National Natural Science Foundation of China Management Science Department Major Project and the National Natural Science Foundation of China International Cooperation Research Project.

出版与奖项

Dr. Li has published SCI/SSCI papers in renowned journals including Omega - International Journal of Management Science, Applied Energy, and Sustainable Cities and Society. He was awarded the Third Prize of Hubei Province Social Science Outstanding Achievement and was listed in the 2022 Global Top 2% Scientists Ranking.
